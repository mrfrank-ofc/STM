About St. Mary's High School

St. Mary's High School is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education. Founded in 1950, we have been nurturing young minds to become leaders of tomorrow. Our school offers a wide range of academic programs, including Sciences, Arts, and Commercials, as well as a variety of extracurricular activities to ensure the all-round development of our students.
